Using the Site:




To create an account, fill in the required fields, and press "Sign Up". This will make your account live and you can start using the platform. Your email will not be public or stored, it is just used for verification. If you do not feel comfotable putting your full name, you may put just your first.



Group Chatting:



To join a group channel, ask the channel owner to add you. This can be done through the "Info" tab on the creators screen. You can then send messages to all recipients, including pings. You can also send emojis with the emoji button. To send a gif through the giffy platform, type a backslash \ followed by gify to send a random gif. To specify a gif, type a gif description like "Happy". You can also pin messages, reply to messages and delete messages. You can also flag messages, and mute people.



Private Messages



To send private messages, Click the plus on the Direct Messages tab, and type a username or name, and click on it. They will automatiaclly be added to your chats. This is 100% private. No one can see your messages, read or delete them. They are E2EE (End to End Encrypted) for privacy.



Uploading Files



To upload a file, click on the attachment icon in the message bar and upload a file. There is technically no limit to how big the file could be, but please dont let it exceed 15-20MB, otherwise the backend hosting service might get, annoyed.



Well, thats basically all there is to it. There will be more features coming down the road, like notifications, etc. but for now, it works and thats what matters. If you find bugs (which im sure you will), send them to Admin, along with any other complintsm issues or feature requests you want.
